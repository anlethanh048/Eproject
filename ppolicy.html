Park 's Regulations

  1. Visitors are requested to contact reception for information, services and entrance tickets.
  2. Our guides are held responsible for helping visitors in all activities.
  3. No changes to the exiting landscapes should be attempted.
  4. Toxic chemicals, explosives, inflammable should not be introduced to the park. Fire burning, tree felling, branch cutting, littering and hunting are not allowed in park.
  5. The collection of plant, animal, insect mineral specimen samples and other relevant objects for scientific research should be permitted by competent authorities
  6. Children under 12 years old must be accompanied by an adult during the zoo visit.
  7. Do not climb, cross walls, median strips specified in Zoo.
  8. Clean up the garbage after the picnic.
