Welcome to the at Home
Wildlife !

The Home Wildlife (also known as The Zoological Park and Gardens) is the largest and oldest zoological garden in Viet Nam and the only zoo at the Sai Gon. The unique climate in Sai Gon allows us to keep a wide variety of animals from Asia, Australia, and Africa, representing a broad mix of species that is unlike any other zoo in the country
