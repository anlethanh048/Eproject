Tell us what you think

Jan B

OMG!! One of my favorite experiences EVER!! Not a “zoo”— it’s a paradise! I highly recommend paying the $$ to get the “caddy tour” if you’re not up to walking a lot— it’s a huge property! We had a darling, knowledgeable guide who took us EVERYWHERE

GrandmaBB1

Arrived not long after opening and didn't have to line up to get in. Staff were friendly and knowledgeable. Well worth the visit, after years of wanting to visit, we finally had the chance to and we were not disappointed, It is very big and its just so well designed for the beautiful animals they have there.

Mavis C

What an incredible day! This would have to be the best zoo in the world. Easy to get to and from by local bus. Staff very friendly and helpful. Great to see staff walking around with animals/birds. The shuttle bus was good once your legs get tired but you want to keep going. Pack a picnic in your backpack.

Maureen S

A very clean and well set out Zoo with good free parking close to the entrance. The free water fountains to refill your drink bottles and the hand sanitisers around the grounds a great idea. Beautiful grounds and most of the animal enclosures were well set up. Friendly staff, as was expected food was a tad expensive.
